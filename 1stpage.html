WHY "CAR"??

Well you may know what is car and might seen it many a time in your life but did you ever wonder why do we call it "car" only and not anything else.
So let explore it,actually the English word car is believed to originate from Latin carrus/carrum "wheeled vehicle" or (via Old North French) Middle English carre "two-wheeled cart", both of which in turn derive from Gaulish karros "chariot". It originally referred to any wheeled horse-drawn vehicle, such as a cart, carriage, or wagon. "Motor car," attested from 1895, is the usual formal term in British English".Autocar", a variant likewise attested from 1895 and literally meaning "self-propelled car," is now considered archaic. "Horseless carriage" is attested from 1895.
Now you know why is it called so.
